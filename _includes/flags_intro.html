Troop 478 Flag Fundraiser

For  Spring Valley Village, Hilshire Village, Brykerwoods,
and the Springwood/Timber Creek neighborhoods

Support Troop 478 and have a beautiful US Flag posted in your front yard for

For a minimum donation of $40.00, BSA Troop 478 will post a 3’x5’  U.S. flag on an 8’ pole in your front yard for Memorial Day, Independence Day, and Veterans Day.

The Boy Scouts will set up the flags before the holiday and take them down afterwards.  All you have to do is enjoy the patriotic effect of the flags waving up and down the streets.

 Sign up now to make sure your home is included in the flag holidays.  All proceeds from this service will benefit the Boy Scouts in Troop 478.
